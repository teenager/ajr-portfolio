Capital Canaries

Voluntary position, ongoing

The Capital Canaries are a London based supporters group for Norwich City Football Club. Founded back in 1975, it's one of the oldest supporters groups still active. Providing discouted group travel to all home and away games, ongoing fanzine, social events throughout the year which are ocassionally attended by representatives from the football club, this group of over 200+ paid members is a great way to meet fellow supporters based in London. I've been part of the committee for a number of years now, which is run on a voluntary basis. From running away travel, designing the fanzine to rebuilding the website, I'm glad to be a part of this supporters group. All of this work was carried out in my spare time across a number of seasons.

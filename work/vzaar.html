Vzaar – Video Hosting Platform

April 2010–April 2013

I moved back to London at the beginning of April 2010 to take up the role of Web Designer at Vzaar, a video hosting platform where users can upload and manage their video content to embed on their own site. Working within the companies brand guidelines, my initial focus was to create clean informative landing pages to convert visitors into paying customers as well as working with the marketing team to produce ad banners, for use with Adwords campaigns. As I became more senior, I learnt more about how to enhance the user experience of a product. I started by redesigning the user admin interface, which gives users a way to customise their content, manage their videos and creating bespoke video pages.

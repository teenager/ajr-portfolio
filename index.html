Aaron Rodgers – UI Designer

Welcome to my portfolio. I'm currently based in Berlin, having most recently worked for Songkick in London.

I've been working in the digital industry for over 9 years after graduating from the University of Lincoln in 2007 with a degree in Interactive Design.

This bit is all about me

Born and raised in the small Suffolk village of Corton, I managed to escape and attended the University of Lincoln where I graduated with a degree in Interactive Design. After which, I was naive and moved straight to London where I thought I'd land a job right away. I didn't, and I ended up in Salisbury. Once I'd gained experience in Wiltshire, I headed back to London in 2010 where I've been working as a UI Designer up until January 2017, when I relocated to my favourite city, Berlin.

I'm proficent with tools such as Sketch, Photoshop, Lightroom, confident with HTML, CSS and have a working knowledge of application frameworks such as Ruby on Rails. I also enjoy the challenge of building email templates, I know, right!?

Design aside, I am a passionate football supporter and can often be found travelling around the UK following Norwich City. I'm a keen photographer, focusing on sports, landscapes and travel photography. Buying records makes me happy, especially rare ones.
